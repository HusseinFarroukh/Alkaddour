Custom clearance

As a customs broker, AL Kaddour Company assists importers and exporters in processing shipment declarations through interconnected border and customs agencies. It also prepares the required documents, calculates duties and other taxes on behalf of the importer, arranges required checks by government agencies, and arranges delivery.

ABOUT US Welcome To Al Kaddour

Al-Kaddour Shipping Company was established in 2021 in Turkey with remarkable fast progress and positive results during this period.
It offers freight forwarding of goods from Turkey to Lebanon and from Lebanon to Turkey, with sea, air and land transportation services, as well as trolleys and counters freight. In addition to clearing customs transactions in Mersin, Beirut and Tripoli, and shipping to Syria, Iraq and all Arab countries.
